FAQs

What is your mode of payment?

Currently, we only accept payments made through online bank transfers. PayPal and Gcash payments are also accepted. From time to time, we offer our cash on delivery service.

How much is the shipping fee?

Your delivery address will determine your shipping cost. Our base rate for Cagayan de Oro is 60 pesos, while the base rate for provinces starts at 190 pesos. If your item weighs more than our standard shipping weight, a 50 Php fee is added.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! We ship worldwide, so if you live outside of the Philippines and are interested in our products, don't hesitate to place your order on our website. Please be aware that as the weight increases, the shipping fee increases as well. Thank you!
